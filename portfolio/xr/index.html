  <p>Immersive and 360 projects migrated from my former Wix portfolio.</p>

  <p><strong>Motel Sunshine Spec Intro</strong> (Oct 28, 2024) - A concept intro for a potential episodic VR world, built to establish tone, visual language, and audience positioning for immersive storytelling. The piece acts as a proof-of-direction for a serialized experience format. <a href="https://vimeo.com/1024206784">watch</a> | <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/motel-sunshine-spec-intro">archive page</a></p>

  <p><strong>Ride The Dice</strong> (Nov 28, 2023) - An immersive music experience developed around spatial presence, rhythm, and environmental storytelling. The project explores how music video language can be expanded into a more interactive, multi-perspective format. <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n8BOpz_l-Lw">watch</a> | <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/ridethedice">archive page</a></p>

  <p><strong>Conflicting Perspectives</strong> (Apr 27, 2023) - A 360 film experiment created for immersive research, focused on point-of-view tension and spatial framing. The work tests how perspective shifts can influence interpretation when the viewer controls where to look. <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZSeG-sg0ZYQ&t=1s">watch</a> | <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/conflictingperspectives">archive page</a></p>

  <p><strong>Falling. (Apart)</strong> (Apr 17, 2020) - An early 360 project grounded in artistic realism and emotional atmosphere. The piece investigates how immersion can heighten vulnerability, scale, and embodied feeling inside a cinematic frame without fixed edges. <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-CvGL98UYpo">watch</a> | <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/fallingapart">archive page</a></p>
