  <h1>Films</h1>
  <p>selected film work moved over from my former Wix portfolio.</p>

  <p><strong>NYU Master's Thesis Short Film</strong> (May 31, 2025) - "Wolfe & The Bee" was my graduate thesis film, shot entirely on the LED soundstage at the Martin Scorsese Institute of Virtual Production in Brooklyn, NY. i used this project to push narrative direction while learning how to shape worlds in camera through virtual production. <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i9nhfqqDeuY">watch</a> | <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/nyu-master%27s-thesis-short-film">archive page</a></p>

  <p><strong>No End For Sight</strong> (Apr 28, 2025) - an AI-native short film where i explored workflow, authorship, and tone inside a fully synthetic pipeline. i built it to test how far concept, pacing, and emotional continuity can go with AI-generated image and sequence work. <a href="https://youtu.be/HNyakg44Qao">watch</a> | <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/no-end-for-sight">archive page</a></p>

  <p><strong>A Fall At The Circus</strong> (Sep 30, 2024) - a 30-second animated short created and rendered in Maya, focused on stylized storytelling and motion timing. it is a compact narrative moment where i leaned into composition, look development, and finish quality. <a href="https://reisjgordon.wixsite.com/reis-gordon/portfolio-1/a-fall-at-the-circus">archive page</a></p>
