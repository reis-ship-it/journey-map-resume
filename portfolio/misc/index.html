  <h1>misc.</h1>
  <p><strong>Bonsai + gardening:</strong> I treat bonsai and gardening as a long-term practice in patience, attention, and care. Pruning, wiring, repotting, and planning seasonal growth cycles keep me grounded, while the day-to-day maintenance reminds me that good design is often quiet, gradual work.</p>

  <p><strong>Baking + cooking:</strong> In the kitchen, I enjoy the balance of precision and improvisation. Baking teaches me discipline through ratios, timing, and technique; cooking lets me explore intuition and adaptation. Both practices sharpen my ability to iterate, taste, and refine until something feels complete.</p>

  <p><strong>Other art practices:</strong> Outside my main mediums, I explore small studies in mixed media, visual experiments, and craft-based projects that keep curiosity active. These side practices help me test new ideas quickly and often feed directly back into my larger creative work.</p>
