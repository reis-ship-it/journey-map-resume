  <h1>misc.</h1>
  <p><strong>Bonsai + gardening:</strong> i like to garden and grow things because it is something easy to take care of, and it helps me reflect on myself. growth really can just be from a glass of water. Bonsai is new for me. I inherented my grandfathers trees and have been taking care of them, nursing them back to health. It is intersting, how bonsai allows us to see a large thing smaller, like a miniture mastery and shaping of nature - something i dare never do in reality.</p>

  <p><strong>Baking + cooking:</strong> when i make anything to eat I never take the time to truly measure, but I always prepare for what can be. When I cook I imagine myself and those who are with me enjoying (or not) the dish or treat. That is what guides me as I mix, stir, chop, fold, saute, bake, grill, flambe, anything really. I let my senses of taste and smell guide me in the cooking phase, plus whispers of a happy future where everyone can enjoy something together. it often works out, but when it doesnt i get to improve my senses.</p>

  <p><strong>Other art practices:</strong> I like to tinker with a bunch of stuff, i'll see if it interests me and usually it does. this is just some stuff i've made in touch designer, maya, photoshop, unreal engine, and any other electronic art thing i'ev played with.</p>
